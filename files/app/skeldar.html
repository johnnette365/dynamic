The SJ V-200 is a fully autonomous VTOL Unmanned Aerial System (UAS) that has been proven both in maritime and land operations. The SJ V-200 UAS has primarily been selected due to its superior design for advanced ship-based and land operations. The SJ V-200 UAS has, during the last 3 years, been chosen by multiple NATO countries. The German Navy, Belgium Navy and Dutch Navy are currently under contract for their respective programs to provide remote surveillance capability.
The SJ V-200 platform is a best-in-class rotary winged medium-range tactical UAS for day and night operations. It can be equipped with multiple sensor payloads such as High-Definition Electro Optical and Infra-Red (EO/IR) sensor, LiDAR systems, AESA SAR RADAR, AIS etc.
